```flow
start=>start: 接收到消息
info=>operation: 读取信息
setCache=>operation: 更新缓存
end=>end: 处理结束

start->info->setCache->end
```



```flow
start=>start: API请求
cache=>operation: 读取Redis缓存
cached=>condition: 是否有缓存？
sendMq=>operation: 发送MQ，后台服务更新缓存
info=>operation: 读取信息
setCache=>operation: 保存缓存
end=>end: 返回信息

start->cache->cached
cached(yes)->sendMq
cached(no)->info
info->setCache
setCache->end
sendMq->end

```
